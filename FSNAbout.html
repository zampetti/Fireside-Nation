Welcome to Fireside Nation

The All-American Radio Show

The Fireside Nation Project

The Fireside Nation Project is a crowdsourced oral history project that investigates the process of history-making through close relationships, identity, and memory. We want to get to know the nuts and bolts of state and local communities, which means we want to get to know you. We'd like to build an archive of your stories, no matter who you are or where you come from, and map out the identity of America from coast to coast. So grab a microphone, download a recording app, and start making history!

The Process

  • Monthly Topics

    Every month, we will introduce a new topic that will prompt you to think about who you are, where you are, and what experiences you've had that got you there. The topic will usually cover the places you call home, the people who inspire you, what it is that you know about them, and what they know about you. We don't want to just hear your story, we want you to engage with others, talk to each other, and make your story come to life.

  • Submissions

    We will design a list of questions that will reflect that month's topic. You will be prompted to fill out short questionnaires or surveys, but best of all, we want to actually hear from you! Each segment will give you the option to upload an audio file that you yourself will create. Answer the questions in any way you wish, we only ask that you respect the integrity of the site, and have fun!

    Call your Mother...
    The Call your Mother segment isn't just sound advice, it is the soul of the project. While your input is invaluable, we'd really like to get some context behind your story. Of course, you don't have to call your mother; you could call another parent, a grandparent, a sibling, or even a good friend. Whoever you choose, we hope you'll feel free to express yourself openly for what we hope will be a long-term project, where voices from all over America will be heard for years to come.

  • Breaking it All Down

    After we've collected your submissions, we will provide a breakdown of what we've heard, and give you a chance to comment.
